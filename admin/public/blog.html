When Should I Service My Car?

March 2026

Most manufacturers recommend servicing at least once a year or every 10,000–12,000 miles. Regular servicing reduces breakdown risk and keeps your vehicle running safely.

How to Check Your Tyre Tread

February 2026

The legal minimum tread depth in the UK is 1.6mm across the central 75% of the tyre. Use a 20p coin to check — if the outer band is visible, it's time for new tyres.
